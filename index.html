About Craft Hub Toronto

Craft Hub Toronto is a creative space dedicated to preserving and sharing traditional wood carving skills. We believe in the power of making things with your hands.

Our workshops are intentionally small—usually 6 to 8 participants—so you get personal attention and a relaxed, focused learning environment.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, our experienced instructors will guide you through each step of the process.
